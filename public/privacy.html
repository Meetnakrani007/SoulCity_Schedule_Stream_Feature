Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoulCity By EchoRP ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and services. We are committed to transparency regarding our data practices.

This application uses YouTube API Services to display publicly available livestream information. By using this application, you are also agreeing to be bound by the Google Privacy Policy.

2. YouTube API Services

This application uses YouTube API Services to fetch and display publicly available YouTube data. Specifically, we access the following types of public data through the YouTube Data API v3:

  • Video Titles — The title of upcoming livestreams
  • Video Thumbnails — Thumbnail images associated with videos
  • Scheduled Start Times — When a livestream is scheduled to begin
  • Channel Names — The name of the YouTube channel hosting the stream
  • Video IDs — Used to link directly to YouTube watch pages
  • Published Dates — When the video listing was created

All data displayed on this site is publicly available on YouTube and is fetched in real-time through official YouTube API endpoints. We do not access any private, unlisted, or user-specific YouTube data.

For more information about YouTube's terms and Google's privacy practices, please visit:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

3. Data Collection

We want to be completely transparent: we do not collect, store, or process any personal user data.

  • We do not require user accounts or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers from visitors
  • We do not store any YouTube data in our own databases — all data is fetched in real-time and displayed directly
  • We do not track user behavior or browsing activity
  • We do not share any data with third parties

4. How Data is Used and Displayed

The publicly available YouTube data we fetch is used solely to:

  • Display a gallery of upcoming Soul City GTA RP livestreams
  • Show stream titles, thumbnails, channel names, and scheduled times
  • Provide direct links to watch streams on YouTube

All YouTube data is displayed as-is from the YouTube API without modification, derivation, or calculation. We do not create derived metrics or alter YouTube data in any way.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

We do not use local storage or session storage to persist any user data.

Third-party services loaded on this site (such as Google Fonts and Font Awesome CDN) may use their own cookies or tracking mechanisms as governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy (for Google Fonts)
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy (for cdnjs / Font Awesome CDN)

6. No Derived Data

We do not create, compute, or display any metrics derived from YouTube data. All information shown on this site comes directly from the YouTube Data API v3 and is presented without modification.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to YouTube, Discord, and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. We encourage you to review each service's privacy policy.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Revoking Access

Since this application does not require any user authentication or access to personal YouTube accounts, there is no data access to revoke. However, you can manage your Google account permissions at any time via the Google Security Settings page.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
